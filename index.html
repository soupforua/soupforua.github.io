State of Ukraine

Across the Ukraine, communities are facing intense struggles. 8 months of conflict has exhausted everyone. In many places unemployment is over 50% while key supplies like water, electricity, heating oil, gasoline and housing materials are in short supply.

All this while millions remain under the threat of daily violence from the Russian military.

Our Mission

Operation 'Soup for UA' is providing warn soup, key supplies like water, childcare products, feminine products, non-perishable foods and more. We are coordinating with various NGOs to distribute vital goods to those most in need.

Get Involved

Soup Nation is made possible through monetary support.

We are accepting limited monetary support for gas, food purchases, salaries for our local Ukrainian workers and supplies, all critical towards the mission.
