Soup Nation|UA

is an independent, humanitarian response initiative to provide support to Ukrainians impacted by the ongoing conflict.

  • Get Started

State of Ukraine

Across the south and east of Ukraine, local economies have all but collapsed. Over 6 months of conflict has exhausted everyone. Unemployment is higher than employment while key supplies like water, electricity, heating oil and housing materials are in short supply. Millions remain under the threat of daily violence from the Russian occupation.

Our Mission

Soup Nation’s work is focused on operating a mobile soup kitchen providing hot soup, fresh bread and key supplies like water, childcare products, feminine products, non-perishable foods and more. We will be coordinating with various NGOs to distribute vital goods to those most in need. Our team has sourced a mini-bus from Poland and will be purchasing many of our supplies inside Ukraine (supporting the UA economy).

Get Involved

Soup Nation is seeking local donations of kitchen supplies to directly support our efforts.
If you’re in Warsaw, Poland we have an up-to-date list of needed kitchen items: here

We are accepting limited monetary support for gas, food purchases and supplies is critical towards the mission. Donations will be accepted in the coming days.
